Meet Your Marketing Specialist

Every Northside Network Provider is assigned a dedicated primary marketing contact known as the Marketing Specialist. These highly skilled and versatile professionals are equipped to offer customized marketing services specifically tailored to meet the unique needs of each practice.

To ensure that we capture all requests accurately, please follow these steps:

  1. Download and Complete a Project Request Form: You can find the form on the marketing intranet. This form is essential for capturing all the necessary details about your request.
  2. Submit the Form: Once completed, send the request form to marketingrequests@northside.com. Upon receipt, it will be assigned to the appropriate team for further action.

By working closely with your designated Marketing Specialist, you can ensure that your practice’s marketing initiatives are both effective and well-coordinated.

How to submit request forms

1. Download appropriate form from the Marketing Intranet.

2. Digitally complete the form with all relevant details.

3. Email completed form to marketingrequests@northisde.com.

Role & Responsibilities of A Marketing Specialist

  • Lead Marketing Specialist (Primary Point of Contact):
  • Drive strategic planning and execution for the primary service line.
  • Serve as the project lead for all service line and practice-related marketing requests.
  • Facilitate regular meetings with stakeholders to ensure alignment and progress (at least monthly).
  • Organize regular internal team meetings to review priorities and adjust strategies (at least monthly).
  • Cultivate and manage relationships with content and sponsorship partners.
  • Act as the primary liaison for all stakeholder interactions, ensuring clear communication and effective collaboration.

Secondary MS (inform on emails/ attend meetings):

  • Support the Lead Marketing Specialist in strategic planning and execution for the main service line.
  • Assist with managing service line and practice-related marketing requests.
  • Stay informed on key communications and updates by monitoring relevant emails.
  • Participate in stakeholder meetings to ensure consistent messaging and alignment (at least monthly).
  • Attend regular internal team meetings to stay updated on priorities and provide support (at least monthly).

Design Lead:

  • Develop and maintain style guides and brand guidelines for the assigned service line.
  • Lead the creation and execution of visual branding for all collateral, ensuring it aligns with strategic objectives.
  • Collaborate with the team to incorporate feedback and refine designs based on stakeholder input.
  • Serve as the primary contact for customized collateral requests, ensuring brand consistency across all materials.

Web Lead:

  • Serve as the primary point of contact for all web-related requests within the service line.
  • Provide strategic direction on user experience to enhance the effectiveness of digital platforms.
  • Oversee the implementation of web updates and improvements, ensuring they align with overall marketing objectives.
  • Guide the team on requests related to digital platforms, such as Kyruus and Google My Business (GMB), ensuring optimal online presence and accessibility.
